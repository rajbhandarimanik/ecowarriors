About Eco Warriors

Eco Warriors is a team dedicated to promoting sustainable waste management practices among students.

Our focus is on educating and encouraging students to adopt eco-friendly habits and reduce their environmental footprint through proper waste management.

We value your opinions and suggestions! Please take a moment to fill out our survey below to share your thoughts on sustainable waste management.

Based on your feedback, we plan to organize various events and initiatives to further promote sustainability on campus.
